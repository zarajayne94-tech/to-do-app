Support

Got a question? We've got answers.

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar, fill in your name, email, and a password of at least 8 characters, then hit Create Account.

Are my tasks private?

Yes. Every task is tied to your account and only visible when you're logged in. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.

Can I use To Do App on my phone?

Absolutely. The app is fully responsive and works on any device with a modern browser.

How do I delete a task?

On the dashboard, each task has an × button in the top-right corner. Click it to permanently delete that task.
